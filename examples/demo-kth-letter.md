Thank you for your letter regarding the joint research programme on
formal methods for distributed systems. We have reviewed the proposal
with great interest and are pleased to confirm KTH's participation.

# Project overview

The collaboration will involve three principal investigators from
our School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. We
anticipate the first joint workshop to take place in Stockholm
during the autumn term.
